What I learnt from my experience !

Amazon Cork

Investigation Specialist

My responsibility was checking the amazon seller's documentation to ensure the seller was safe and qualified.

The Amazon culture is amazing and complete. It went through all of the decisions to make sure you work as a leader.

To be a leader, you have to be smart and make the right decisions to serve your customers than simply do what your boss tells you to do.

2015/June/14 -- 2016/July/15

Fudi&more

iOS Engineer

I was the iOS Engineer and co-founder who developed the app from scratch.

Also, I drafted the business plan, helped to make strategic decisions and did a few marketing projects.

As an iOS engineer, I published my first industry app. The code was not perfect but I improved my learning and problem-solving skills.

As a co-founder, I have witnessed the company's build-up. Just like other companies, we did have setbacks. However, our success was how quickly you respond and adapt from setbacks.

2016/July/15 -- 2018/June/1

Boonray Technology

iOS/Android Engineer

As a mobile engineer, I developed android/iOS apps to operate the drone. I regard the drone as the 3rd human eye to see the world from the height we can't reach.

It makes me feel like you are seeing the world on the giants' shoulder. Unfortunately, the drone can not do much work but take pictures. I believe it will be equipped with hands in the future.

I started developing android apps there. I can still remember when the manager told me to develop the android app. I wanted to say no because I didn't know if I could make it. I wanted to say yes because I knew I liked learning new technologies.

Meanwhile, as an observer to see the company making management decisions. I gained a lot of experience and realized how important the culture plays in a company.

2018/Nov/11 -- 2019/Oct/15

Lyne Chemicals LTD

Software Engineer

I started learning python as a freelancer. As I have learnt swift and java, learning python was quick and easy.

It took me a while to adapt to being an API-back-end developer while I handled authentication, okta API, system design and CI/CD. Overall, it's pretty easy to develop Django API and Website.

So far, I believe my client is happy for what I have created. We strictly implemented scrum development. We had good communication throughout the process which ensured we were in the right direction.
